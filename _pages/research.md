My interest in language contact was intially sparked by my fascination on how multiple languages coexist in time and space together. From there, reading works on pronoun borrowing grew my desire to learn more about how contact induces language change. I currently focus on how morphology (including clitics), pronouns, and other linguistic features can change when two or more languages interact. This has implications for bi- and multilingualism, creolistics, and language genesis and evolution. Ultimately, I think language contact can tell us so much about humankind's past, present, and future.
